# Business Requirement Document (BRD)

## Project Title
Global E-commerce Sales Analytics & Business Insights

## Target Audience
Executive Leadership, Sales Operations, Marketing Teams

## Core Business Questions
1. **Revenue & Profitability:** Which product categories, regions, and customer segments drive the highest margin vs. lowest margin?
2. **Sales Operations:** Where are the logistical bottlenecks (shipping delays, high discount rates impacting profit)?
3. **Customer Value:** Who are our highest-value customers, and what are their buying behaviors?

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Financial:** Total Revenue, Total Profit, Profit Margin (%), Average Order Value (AOV).
* **Operational:** Average Shipping Duration (Days), Return/Cancellation Rate (%).
* **Customer:** Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), Order Frequency, Recency.
